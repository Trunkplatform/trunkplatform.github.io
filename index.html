Vertical not Horizontal

In contrast to a ‘horizontal’ approach focused on trying to solve a business problem generically, we want to embrace the complexity with customised solutions that bring real value to a particular domain. We can provide one platform for an industry that covers all of their technical requirements – so there’s no need for multiple products with many options that are never used.
